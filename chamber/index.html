About the Chamber

The Bragg-Creek Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to promoting local businesses, organizing community events, and providing networking opportunities for its members. Our mission is to foster a thriving business environment in Bragg-Creek and support the growth and success of our members.

History

The Bragg-Creek Chamber of Commerce was founded in 1985 by a group of local business owners who wanted to create a unified voice for the business community. Since then, the Chamber has grown to include over 100 members and has become an integral part of the Bragg-Creek community.

Membership

The Bragg-Creek Chamber of Commerce is a dedicated organization that supports and promotes local businesses, fosters community collaboration, and drives economic growth in the Bragg-Creek area.

Discover Bragg-Creek

Bragg-Creek is a small town located in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains. It is home to many outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and skiing. The town has a population of about 1,500 people and is known for its beautiful scenery and friendly locals.
